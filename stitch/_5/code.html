Broccoli Mac & Cheese

A sneaky way to get those greens in. Creamy, cheesy, and full of little trees.

schedule 25 mins group Serves 4

grocery Ingredients

  • Macaroni
    2 cups
  • Broccoli
    1 head
  • Cheddar
    1.5 cups
  • Milk
    1 cup

list_alt Steps

1

Boil water in a large pot. Add macaroni and cook according to package instructions. In the last 3 minutes, add the chopped broccoli florets.

2

Drain the pasta and broccoli well and return to the warm pot off the heat.

3

Stir in milk and shredded cheddar cheese until completely melted and creamy. Season to taste.
